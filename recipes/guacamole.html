Guacamole

Add a twist to your nachos!

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Vegetables (onions and tomatoes)
  • Fresh cilantro, minced garlic, cayenne pepper

Steps

      Smash the avocado in a bowl
      Add lime juice, salt and chopped vegetables
      Add herbs and spices
      Mix all together
