Pesto Pasta

My favorite dish to prepare after a long day of work

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner.

This versatile pesto pasta recipe is easy to customize by adding your favorite protein, if you like. Chicken, sausage and chickpeas are all delicious mix-ins.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion and oil
  • Pesto
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings
  3. Add the pesto mix to the hot pasta and toss with cheese
